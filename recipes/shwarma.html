Shwarma

Description

Shawarma is a Levantine dish that originated in the Ottoman Empire. It consists of meat that is cut into thin slices, stacked in an inverted cone, and roasted on a slow-turning vertical spit. Traditionally made with lamb or mutton, it may also be made with chicken, turkey, beef, or veal.
